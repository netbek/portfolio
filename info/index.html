What I do

2016⁠–2023

At Siyavula Education, I helped to create high-quality learning experiences in Mathematics and Physical Sciences, and monitoring and evaluation tools for teachers.

As lead front-end developer, I set the technical direction for the front-end stack, provided engineering perspective in high-level product meetings, and explored new approaches and technologies. I created and maintained UI components, guided and reviewed front-end work by the full-stack team, and did QA testing.

For 5 years, I also did full-stack development, specialising in data analytics. I designed and implemented reports and dashboards for classroom and internal use, and reviewed and productionised contributions by the data analysis team.

  • Languages: CSS, HTML, JavaScript (browser, Node.js), Python, SQL
  • Databases: PostgreSQL
  • Frameworks and libraries: Flask, Ionic, jQuery, Pandas, Pyramid, React, Redux, Tailwind, Vega
  • Design systems and tools: Balsamiq, Figma, Material Design
  • Version control: Git
  • Containers: Docker

2002⁠–2015

I was a freelance web developer and organised creative swapmeets that combined academia, art, and free culture.

I worked solo and in agency teams, and often took on multiple roles as designer, front- and back-end developer, and technical support. I built and maintained websites of online and print magazines, broadcast media consultants, radio stations, an art gallery, an architecture firm, an event agency, guest houses, and various other small businesses. I also worked on integrated marketing campaigns for PUMA, BlackBerry, Standard Bank, Castrol, Spur, 5FM, Metro FM, and other large brands.

  • Languages: ActionScript, CSS, HTML, JavaScript (browser, Node.js), PHP, SQL
  • Databases: MySQL
  • Frameworks and libraries: Adobe Flash, AngularJS, Bootstrap, Drupal, jQuery, Kohana
  • Design tools: Adobe Fireworks
  • Version control: Git
